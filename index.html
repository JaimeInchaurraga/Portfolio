Exploring Video Game Trends. Pandaland is a data-driven exploration project where I analyzed video game trends in 2023. By scraping data from platforms like Backloggd and IGDB, I built a comprehensive dataset of over 50,000 video games and performed in-depth data analysis. The project revealed key insights into popular genres, top companies, platform dominance, and factors driving game success.
